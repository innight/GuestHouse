Who are We? What We Do?

The increase in the variety of the standardized offer and the loss of the original and typical of a country in terms of tourism, as well as the comparison between what is already done in other countries, has led to the identification of the concept, GLOCAL Tourism – a global scope and a local presence. The core business is to allow experiences more adjusted and customized to the reality of the region, Ericeira.

Ericeira has been growing as a village and tourist landmark. One of the driving forces behind this growth was the consecration of Ericeira as a World Surfing Reserve. There are 4 kms of coast that covers the Ericeira World Surfing Reserve, the only one in Europe, and second in the world, and includes seven world-class waves.
