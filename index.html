5 Things to Do in Ericeira!

1- World Surfing Reserve – The only one in Europe! The Ericeira World Surfing Reserve extends between the Empa and São Lourenço beaches, on a coastal belt that covers 7 world-class waves in a space of only 4 km: Pedra Branca, Reef, Ribeira d’Ilhas, Cave, Crazy Left, Coxos and São Lourenço.

2 – Go to Furnas place, near the main hotel, and see the old seafood nurseries. It is in the seafront of Parque de Santa Marta, where you can play mini-golf, see exhibitions, skate or just relax. The nurseries, nowadays they are obsolete, but in ancient times this place was where they breed the lobsters and sea urchins that served all the village restaurants. But, please be careful with the ocean and the rocks!

3- The fisherman´s harbour. At summer time this is a very sought place, since theirs no waves here and it’s perfect for kids. It was from this harbour that the last Portuguese king fled in exile to Brazil with the beginning of the Republic period. Find the many cats that live there from the fresh fish that comes everyday. Then go for a lunch at the seafood restaurants that are specialised in the freshly caught fish

4- The traditional town centre of cobbled streets and whitewashed houses. And visit the many fountains and make wishes!

5- Just sit, relax and enjoy the amazing good energy of Ericeira!
